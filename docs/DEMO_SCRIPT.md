# Haven — Demo Video Script & Shot List

**Target length:** 2:45 (hard cap 3:00). Judges are time-boxed; every second earns its place.
**Format:** Screen recording of the live app with a calm, confident voiceover. No slides. No logo intro. Real Google account, real data, real-time syncing visible on screen.
**Goal:** Defend the two heaviest-weighted criteria — Problem Solving & Impact (20%) and Agentic Depth (20%) — by *showing* the proactive brain act unprompted, not just describing features.

> Recording tip: Do a dry run first and seed a realistic-but-messy week so the brain-dump and the proactive nudges have real material to work with. Keep the cursor calm; no frantic clicking.

---

## Beat sheet (timed)

### 0:00 – 0:18 — The pain, stated in one breath
**On screen:** Your face for 3 seconds (optional) or straight to the app's calm landing/dashboard.
**Voiceover:**
> "Every week I overcommit, forget a deadline, and end up scrambling at the last minute. Reminder apps don't fix that — they just nag. So I built Haven: a productivity companion that actually plans and protects my time before things break."

*Why: names the exact problem statement ("Last-Minute Life Saver") in plain human language. No jargon.*

---

### 0:18 – 1:05 — The magic loop (brain-dump → structured plan)
**On screen:** Open the chat / quick-capture. Type a messy, natural-language week:
> "Dentist Tuesday 3pm, finish the client report by Friday, gym 3x this week, mom's birthday Saturday, prep for Monday's interview."

Then show Haven parsing it live into tasks, calendar events, and habits.
**Voiceover:**
> "I just tell Haven my week the way I'd say it out loud. It uses Gemini to turn that into structured tasks, real calendar events, and recurring habits — instantly. No forms, no dragging things around."

*Why: this is your wow moment. Lead with it. Show real Google Calendar entries appearing.*

---

### 1:05 – 1:55 — Agentic depth (the part that wins)
**On screen:** Trigger a proactive intervention. Best options, in priority order:
1. **Overcommitment catch** — a day with more committed hours than available; Haven flags it and offers to redistribute.
2. **Deadline trajectory warning** — the report won't fit remaining free time; Haven proposes a focus block.
Show the one-tap action resolving it. Then open the **"What Haven knows about you"** memory panel.
**Voiceover:**
> "Here's what makes Haven different. I didn't ask for this — it noticed on its own that Thursday is overcommitted and offered a fix in one tap. It's watching my deadlines, my patterns, and my energy. And it *learns*: it remembers when I actually get deep work done and plans around it. The more I use it, the more it understands how *I* work."

*Why: this is the Agentic Depth (20%) proof and your Innovation (20%) moat, in one breath. Say the words "I didn't ask for this" and "it learns."*

---

### 1:55 – 2:30 — Google stack + Auto-Pilot
**On screen:** Show real integrations working: a Google Calendar event syncing, a task completing in Google Tasks, and Auto-Pilot planning the day. Optionally show a Slides outline being generated.
**Voiceover:**
> "Everything runs on Google — Gemini for the intelligence, and native Calendar, Tasks, Gmail, and Slides through the Model Context Protocol. I can even hand Haven the wheel: Auto-Pilot plans my entire day around my real commitments and the habits I've built."

*Why: bags the Usage of Google Technologies (15%) score explicitly and shows breadth without touring every page.*

---

### 2:30 – 2:45 — The close (the line judges repeat)
**On screen:** Return to a calm, organized dashboard. End card with the **live URL + GitHub link**.
**Voiceover:**
> "Reminder apps tell you what's due. Haven makes sure it gets done — and learns your rhythm so tomorrow is easier than today. That's the difference between a to-do list and a companion."

*Why: one memorable, quotable line. End on the moat, not a feature list.*

---

## Hard rules
- **Show, don't narrate features.** Never read a bullet list on camera.
- **Depth over breadth.** Do NOT tour all 7 pages. Pick the 3 moments above and make them land.
- **Real data, real time.** Let Google syncing happen on screen — that's your proof it's real.
- **Unlisted YouTube** so it stays accessible through evaluation but isn't public.
- **Link it at the top** of the Google Doc and the README.
- **Keep the live app healthy** — the video supplements the deployment, it does not replace it.

## Pre-record checklist
- [ ] Seed a realistic messy week the night before.
- [ ] Confirm the proactive nudge will actually fire (create an overcommitted day).
- [ ] Test OAuth + Calendar/Tasks sync once, live, before recording.
- [ ] Close notifications, hide bookmarks bar, use a clean browser profile.
- [ ] Record at 1080p+; keep voiceover levels consistent.
- [ ] End card includes: live URL, GitHub URL, one-line tagline.
